## Overview  
Created a range of engineering drawings based on CAD models, focusing on clear communication through accurate dimensions and standard drawing practices.

## My Role  
Independently produced all drawings as part of coursework and practice tasks.

## Tools Used  
SolidWorks (drawing tools), and standard dimensioning practices.

## Design & Testing  
Generated 2D drawings from 3D models, applying dimensions, annotations, and views to accurately represent components and assemblies.

## What I Learned  
Improved my ability to communicate designs through technical drawings, including correct dimensioning, layout, and following standard engineering drawing conventions.
