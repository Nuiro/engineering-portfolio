### Overview

Built a custom at-home subwoofer setup, including wiring, power supply integration, and grounding to eliminate noise. Tuned voltage levels and connected a head unit using modified PC and console power supplies.

A custom home audio system built using a car subwoofer and head unit, powered by modified PC and Xbox power supplies.

### My Role
Independently designed, assembled, and tested the entire system, including wiring, power integration, and tuning.

### Tools Used
Electrical wiring, inline resistors, voltmeter/multimeter, PC power supply, Xbox power supply, car head unit, and audio components.

### Design & Testing
Configured and wired multiple power sources, ensuring stable voltage supply and proper grounding to eliminate electrical noise. Tuned amplifier gain and voltage levels to achieve clean output without distortion or overloading components.

### What I Learned
Developed a deeper understanding of power management, grounding techniques, and signal integrity in audio systems. Improved troubleshooting skills by identifying and resolving noise and stability issues.
