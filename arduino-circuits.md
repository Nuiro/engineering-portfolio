### Overview

## What it was 
Designed and built a range of Arduino-based circuits, focusing on controlling and measuring voltage and current using resistive components to meet specific electrical requirements.

## My role
Completed all design, assembly, testing, and programming independently as part of an assessment.

## Tools used  
Breadboard, resistors, Arduino, multimeter, jumper wires, and basic electronic components.

## What I designed/tested  
Constructed circuits to achieve target voltage and current values at specific points, verifying results using a multimeter. Developed simple Arduino code to control and test circuit behaviour.

## What I learned  
Built a strong understanding of circuit fundamentals, including voltage division, resistance, and current flow. Improved accuracy in measurement and troubleshooting, and gained confidence working with both hardware and basic embedded programming.
