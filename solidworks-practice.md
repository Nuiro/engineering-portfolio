## Overview  
Working through a large set of CSWA and CSWP SolidWorks practice problems to develop CAD modelling skills and improve interpretation of technical drawings.

## My Role  
Independently completing all modelling tasks and managing progress through the problem set.

## Tools Used  
SolidWorks (part modelling, assemblies, and drawing tools).

## Design & Testing  
Creating accurate 3D models based on given dimensions and drawings, focusing on correct constraints, feature usage, and efficient modelling techniques. Verifying dimensions and geometry against specifications.

## What I Learned  
Improving accuracy and speed in CAD modelling while developing a stronger understanding of design intent, constraints, and interpreting engineering drawings.
