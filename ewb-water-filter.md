## Overview  
Designed a cost-efficient water filtration system as part of the Engineering Without Borders challenge, aimed at improving access to clean water in rural Timor-Leste.

## My Role  
Took a leading role within the team by coordinating tasks and assigning responsibilities. Managed and refined documentation across Word, PowerPoint, and Excel, and led the SolidWorks design work for the project.

## Tools Used  
SolidWorks, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and engineering design methodologies.

## Design & Testing  
Developed a practical water filtration concept focused on affordability, accessibility, and ease of use. Created CAD models to support the design and effectively communicate the system.

## What I Learned  
Gained experience in leadership and teamwork while working on a real-world problem. Developed a stronger understanding of designing within constraints such as cost, usability, and sustainability.
