## Overview  
Collaboratively designed and developed a mechanical catapult system, including concept development, mathematical analysis, and physical testing. The project required adapting the design to available materials and constraints while achieving consistent launch performance.

## My Role  
Contributed to group brainstorming, design development, and testing. Led the SolidWorks modelling of the catapult components and assembly.

## Tools Used  
SolidWorks, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and basic prototyping materials.

## Design & Testing  
Developed concepts for energy storage and release, incorporating an adjustable radius to control launch distance. Tested and refined the design to improve consistency and performance within given constraints.

## What I Learned  
Gained experience in collaborative engineering design, including idea generation, iteration, and testing. Developed a better understanding of how theoretical concepts translate into physical systems, and how constraints such as materials and adjustability impact real-world performance.
